Systems Programming Ghent

A community for systems programmers to share a passion for performant and reliable software.

What We Do

We aim to bring code enthusiasts together by organising events in Ghent and the surrounding area:

  • Presentations: About personal interests or side-projects in someone's favorite systems programming language. (It does not always have to be Rust, even though it is popular.)
  • Hackathons: Educative coding sessions focused around a challenge with an award for the most beautiful solution at the end.
  • Socials: Informal gatherings such as dinners or drinks to meet and chat with like-minded people within the field. You could for example share your latest discoveries here or find collaborators for your next project.

To register for an event, please contact us.

For Whom

We are searching for developers passionate about performance optimization, security, and formal methods in software development. This could be, for example, hobbyists, students or professionals active in fields such as:

  • Embedded systems
  • Operating systems
  • Compilers
  • Networking
  • Security
  • High-performance computing
  • Robotics

Why We Do It

While the open-source community online is very rich and friendly, people might prefer to meet in-person. Currently, there are not that many groups in Ghent targeted towards back-end or systems developers.

Who We Are

This community was started by a software engineer at OTIV in Ghent (Belgium), a company specialized in software for operating railway vehicles.

This community is still fresh and in need of volunteers. Please contact us if you would like to help with:

  • Creating engaging banners for events
  • Hosting presentations
  • Advertising
  • Contributing presentations
  • ...
